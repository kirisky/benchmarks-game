Background

The benchmarks game comparisons are often criticised and the criticism is often about the crowd-sourced programs being compared.

Some say the programs are too optimised; they use foreign libraries too much and splitters say the programs differ too much to be lumped together.

These are a few of the original mandelbrot programs contributed in 2004 and 2005.

Do they make for a Goldilocks comparison that is just right ?
